Our Laboratories

Explore our world-class laboratories equipped with advanced instrumentation to push the boundaries of scientific discovery.

Short Pulse Laser Laboratory

Specializing in the application of short pulse lasers for medical purposes, including Optical Coherence Tomography, wavelength-tunable confocal fluorescence microscopy, and fluorescence lifetime measurements. We also design and build custom spectrometers, including FTIR systems.

Optical System Design Laboratory

Focused on designing and prototyping optical instruments for medical applications. Our capabilities include computer-aided optical system design, 3D modeling of medical devices, and building prototypes for tissue analysis systems.

Electrochemical Sensors Laboratory

Developing microelectrode arrays using photolithography for advanced electrochemical sensing. We work on detecting disease biomarkers like creatinine, uric acid, and dopamine in biological samples without extensive sample preparation.

HPC and Data Center

Our data center supports high-performance computing for AI-driven data and image analysis. We develop data acquisition systems using online platforms and IoT, and create advanced algorithms for image analysis.

Quantum Sensing Laboratory

Exploring quantum phenomena for ultra-sensitive measurements. Our research includes quantum effects studies, quantum manipulation, single-photon detection, and developing novel quantum sensing technologies.

Optical Fiber Sensing Laboratory

Designing and fabricating microstructured optical fibers for medical sensing applications. We develop prototype systems for producing these fibers and create instruments for measuring biological substance concentrations using surface plasmon resonance.

Photo Therapy Laboratory

Equipped with cell culture facilities to investigate the effects of light on biological systems. This lab supports research into phototherapy applications and light-based treatments.

General Photonics Laboratory

A versatile lab for designing optical measurement tools, electronic circuits, and control software. We develop deep-learning models for agriculture, use hyperspectral imaging for food inspection, and laser speckle imaging for biomedical applications.

Terahertz Imaging System

This system analyzes biofilm coverage on surfaces. It utilizes a Biofilm chamber setup on a cellulose membrane with Time-Domain Terahertz Imaging to detect and analyze biofilm presence.

Thai Rice Classification System

A system designed for the analysis and classification of Thai rice varieties using a Mid-Infrared Dispersive-Spectrometer. It processes the energy reflection signals from the surface of rice grains.

Biological Contamination Detection System

Utilizes separate color image sensors (CMOS for white light and InGaAs for SWIR) to detect biological contamination. The system separates R-G-B and SWIR channels for machine vision applications.

Terahertz-Raman Spectrometer

An advanced instrument for analyzing Raman spectroscopy signals, covering both Stoke and Anti-Stoke ranges. It is also capable of analyzing signals in the Terahertz-Raman range (Raman shift: -200 cm⁻¹ to > 200 cm⁻¹).
