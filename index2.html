Our Vision

To establish a national center of excellence that integrates interdisciplinary expertise to develop and deploy practical photonic technologies, addressing critical challenges in urban sustainability, from public health to environmental monitoring.

Hub of Talents

We unite leading experts from academia, government, and industry to foster collaborative innovation.

Sustainable Cities

Our focus is on creating tangible solutions for smart agriculture, urban safety, and environmental health.

Global Impact

Through international partnerships, we aim to position Thailand as a leader in sustainable urban innovation.

Core Research Areas

Our research is structured around five key pillars designed to enhance the quality of urban life.

Photonics for Aging Society

Developing wearable biosensors, advanced optical imaging, and phototherapy solutions for elderly care.

AI-based Urban Surveillance

Enhancing public safety and security through intelligent, real-time monitoring systems.

Environmental Sensors

Using SPR, Raman, and hyperspectral imaging for precise monitoring of air and water quality.

Smart Agriculture

Integrating Photonics and IoT to improve precision, reduce costs, and promote sustainability in farming.

Industrial Innovations

Modernizing traditional industries and fostering deep-tech startups with novel photonic solutions.
